THE PROBLEM

The original Kaur Institute website faced problems with consistency and did not have a modern design. Our sponsor tasked us with the job of doing a website redesign in hopes of making her site more attractive for new users while still making it easy for her to edit.

MY TASK

Because our team was made up of four Computer Science majors, and two Informatics majors, I was the only one in the group who had experience with web design. I was put in charge of leading the website redesign and making decisions on how the site would look. I mainly helped with creating wireframes and mockups, as well as developing the homepage and site theme.

PROJECT GOALS

After my team had been notified that we would be working on this project, we immediately scheduled a meeting with our sponor in hopes of clarifying project requirements and expectations. After our first meeting, we compiled a list of goals to reach within this short ten week time span:

  • Design a clean, modern website that can appeal to donors, community members, and volunteers.
  • Relay important information and announcements through blog updates.
  • Advertise the different programs available.
  • Improve contact form functionality.
  • Add a way for users to donate to the site.
  • Add links to social media at an appropriate area.
